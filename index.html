Privacy Policy

Files N Folders · Effective July 5, 2026

Files N Folders collects no personal data. Everything you do in the app is processed locally and stays on your device.

Permissions & Data Access

All Files Access: To function as a file manager, the app requires access to browse, manage, copy, move, and delete files across your device storage. This data is only processed locally and never leaves your device.

App Usage Data: The app's Cleanup feature requires access to your app usage statistics to determine which apps are rarely used and calculate their storage size. This data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to any external servers.

All apps access: The app accesses the list of all installed packages to show the installed apps and their storage footprint. This data is never transmitted to any external servers.

Third parties

No third-party SDKs, analytics, or ads are included.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will appear here with a new date.
